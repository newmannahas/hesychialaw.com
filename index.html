                    <h2>Too many lawyers create <span>noise</span>.<br>I create clarity.</h2>

                    <p>Hesychia—from the Greek for stillness, quiet, and peace—represents a revolutionary approach to legal practice.</p>

                    <p>While others escalate, I resolve. While others rely on rhetoric and theatrics, I build leverage through rigor and clear thinking that opponents respect and judges appreciate.</p>

                    <p>My advocacy is built on the classical foundations: <span>logos</span> (logic), <span>ethos</span> (credibility), and <span>pathos</span> (empathy). But soundness of reasoning is the bedrock that makes everything else possible.</p>

                    <p>In the stillness, I see opportunities others miss. I seek win-win solutions that create value beyond the dispute itself.</p>
