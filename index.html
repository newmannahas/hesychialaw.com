Why Hesychia Law

Newman Nahas left behind two decades at the world's most elite law firms for one reason: to focus exclusively on what he loves. And what he does best: Spot the issue that changes everything. Find the argument others miss. Win the cases others won't take.

⛵ Calm Counsel for Turbulent Times

"Hesychia" comes from the Greek for inner calmness, stillness, or peace. And it suggests a new vision for the practice of law. In litigation, storms are inevitable—opposing counsel manufactures crises, deadlines compress, stakes escalate. Most lawyers meet turbulence with more turbulence.

But strength lies in finding the still point at the center of the storm. From there, you see what others miss: the single issue that will determine the outcome, the overlooked precedent that changes everything, the simple truth obscured by complexity.

This is what allows you to rest even while the tempests are raging. This is the Hesychia Law difference.

When the Odds are Long and Stakes High

Handle your hardest appeals. Newman has built a reputation for reversing adverse decisions and preserving crucial victories in state and federal appellate courts. When conventional wisdom says you can't win, that's exactly when you need an unconventional approach. Newman excels at finding the overlooked argument, the novel interpretation, or the fundamental principle that transforms a losing case into a winning one.

An Ounce of Prevention

Build your legal foundations. Strategic positioning before litigation can determine outcomes before a complaint is ever filed. From contract drafting that anticipates disputes to pre-litigation counseling that shapes the battlefield, Newman helps clients win by preventing fights they don't need and preparing for the ones they do.

Cut to the Chase

Develop, draft, and argue your critical motions. Summary judgment, motions to dismiss, and other case-ending strategies—with fees tied to success. Newman focuses on the dispositive issues that can end litigation early and efficiently, saving clients time, money, and uncertainty.

A Second Opinion

Provide fresh perspective on intractable issues. Sometimes you need someone who is not in the weeds to see the forest. When your team has been living with a case for months or years, Newman brings the clarity of distance combined with the insight of experience to spot solutions hiding in plain sight.

Appellate Lawyer at Trial

Go into the trenches with you. Partner with trial counsel—they handle the theatrics, Newman handles the law. By thinking about the appeal from day one of trial, Newman helps create the record that wins not just in the trial court but protects victory on appeal.

Making Peace

Find win-win solutions that grow the pie. These solutions are more common than you might think—what's not common is incentivizing lawyers to find them. Newman's fee structures align with finding resolutions that create value rather than destroy it, turning adversaries into partners where possible.
